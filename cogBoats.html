Cog Boats

They weren't warships.

Cog boats carried warriors in small forts as their method of self defense.

Flags

Each cog boat carried a flag, otherwise known as a Flüger, bearing the colors of the ships hometown.

Later versions

Later versions derived from the Dutch cog boat had three mastes.
