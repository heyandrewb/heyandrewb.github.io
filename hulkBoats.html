Hulk Boats

Manned to the brim

Hulk boats could carry up to appoximately 100 men in each boat.

Named origins

The Hulk boat has been studied to most likely be named after the greek word, holkas, which means towed boat.

Rode on the winds of influence

The hulk boat was largely influenced by the Norse knarr boat, which was the main trade vessel in northern Europe at the time.
