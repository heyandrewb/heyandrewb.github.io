Wind Patterns

Throughout history there has always been a way or form to trade. Some by land and some by sea, Throughout the indian ocean there is always a certain wind pattern that will blow during certain seasons. During the summer there would be winds that head north along the water bank and at they get toward pakistan they would head east still following the waterbank. Whereas during the winter the wind pattern is different. During this time the wind will move the opposite of summer, the wind will move west along the waterbank and then start to move south still along the waterfront.
